# F007 Research

Personas evaluated v1.7.0 against their rubrics — see [team-personas.md](../team-personas.md). The 4 locked items are the highest-confidence interventions from that evaluation.

## Priya (Product Manager / UX) — Cmd+Q injury

> "We measure 'Time to first lesson' but not 'Time to first lesson abandoned mid-flow'. The latter is invisible churn — and shipping a Mac app without crash-safe state is asking for it."

→ Item 1: persistence.

## Lucía (A1–A2 teacher) — pronunciation 4/10, distractor anti-pattern

> "A mis alumnos de A1 no les puedo pedir que escojan entre 'went/gone/going/goes' si apenas vieron 'go' ayer."

→ Item 2 (slow audio modeling) + Item 4 (no `goed` in the option pool).

## James (B1–B2 teacher) — TBLT pivot

Out of scope for v1.8.0 (PO+TL locked). Tracked for v1.10.

## Siobhán (C1–C2 teacher) — corpus depth

Out of scope for v1.8.0 (the simple-past-only ceiling is acknowledged in spec.md, deferred).

## Mariana (Senior product designer) — visual debt

> "`.system(size: 80)` at `accessibility5` overflows the column."

→ Item 3: design tokens + Dynamic Type fix on the two worst offenders.
